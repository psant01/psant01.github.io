External activities - Dr Paul Sant

UK STEM Ambassador

Since 2018 I have been an active ambassador (STEM) via the UK STEM Ambassadoe network. This means that I volunteer to visit school and colleges and to engage with a variety of outreach activities including demos, career talks etc. I really enjoy this, and if you are interested in becoming a UK STEM Ambassador then please visit UK STEM Ambassador website

External examining

As partof my academic role I also engage as an external examiner for a couple of UK Universities. This provides me with insight as to how other institutions approach assessment and learning as well as being able to gather good practice and share thoughts and ideas.

BCS - The Chartered Institute for IT

I am a Fellow of the BCS - The Chartered Institute for IT and engage with their Continuing Profesional Development (CPD) as well as being a member who reviews applications for Chartered IT Professional status.
